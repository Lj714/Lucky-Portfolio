Project Management Services

I help individuals and small businesses plan, organise, and deliver projects from start to finish. My focus is on clarity, structure, and communication so everyone understands what needs to happen and when.

Who I Work With

  • Individuals planning personal or home projects
  • Small businesses managing internal changes or improvements
  • Teams that need help coordinating tasks and timelines

How I Manage Projects

  • Discovery: Understand goals, constraints, and expectations.
  • Planning: Break work into tasks, define owners, and set realistic timelines.
  • Delivery: Track progress, communicate updates, and manage risks or issues.
  • Review: Capture what went well and what can be improved next time.

Tools and Approach

  • Simple plans and trackers using Excel or online tools
  • Clear summaries instead of complex documentation
  • Regular check-ins to keep everyone aligned
