Concept Project • By Lucky Aigbefoh

A simple way to find a reliable handyman when you actually need one.

Instead of searching endlessly or asking around, this concept aims to give people a small, trusted list of handymen they can contact quickly.

Register your interest

How it could work

The idea is to keep things simple for both the person needing help and the handyman providing it.

1. Choose your job type

  • Small repairs
  • Maintenance jobs
  • Furniture assembly

2. See trusted handymen

  • Basic profiles
  • Service area
  • Availability

3. Request contact

  • Send a simple enquiry
  • Share the type of job
  • Get a call or message back

Why this could help

  • Busy people don’t have time to research lots of options.
  • Handymen can receive clearer, more focused enquiries.
  • A small, curated list can build more trust over time.
