Find Your Next Handyman

This project aims to help people connect with reliable handymen for repairs, maintenance, and small improvements around their home or workspace.

Planned Features

  • Categories of handy work (repairs, plumbing, furniture assembly, etc.)
  • A directory or profile list of available handymen
  • Booking or matching system based on location and availability
  • Pricing, service levels, and response time information
