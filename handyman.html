Find Your Next Handyman

This concept project focuses on helping people quickly find reliable handymen for small jobs, repairs, and maintenance around the home or workplace.

The Problem

Many people do not know who to call for small tasks such as fixing a minor issue, assembling furniture, or carrying out basic repairs. They often rely on word of mouth, long online searches, or trial and error.

The Concept

The idea is to provide a simple, trusted place where people can:

  • Choose the type of job they need help with
  • Browse a small list of reliable handymen in their area
  • Send a quick request with basic details of the job

Planned Features

  • Categories for work such as repairs, maintenance, and furniture assembly
  • Short profiles for each handyman with location and services
  • A simple enquiry form to request contact or a quote
  • Potential for ratings or feedback in the future

As my web development skills grow, this could become a small working demo for a handyman-matching service.
